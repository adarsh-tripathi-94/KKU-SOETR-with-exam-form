## üìñ Project Overview
The **K.K. University Feedback Portal** is a centralized web application designed to streamline the collection, categorization, and resolution of student and faculty feedback. It replaces fragmented communication channels with a unified dashboard, ensuring administrative accountability and enhancing the overall campus experience.

---

## ‚ú® Key Features

* **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Distinct dashboards for Students, Faculty, and Administrators.
* **Anonymous Submissions:** Option for students to submit sensitive feedback without revealing their identity.
* **Real-Time Status Tracking:** Users can track whether their feedback is *Pending*, *Under Review*, or *Resolved*.
* **Departmental Routing:** Automated routing of feedback to specific departments (e.g., IT, Maintenance, Academics).
* **Analytics Dashboard:** Visual representation of feedback trends and resolution metrics for university leadership.
